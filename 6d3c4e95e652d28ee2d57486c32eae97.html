Which 7 foods reduce your appetite (and this will help you lose weight faster)?

The question of what to eat to lose weight, periodically asks any of us. For some, this sometimes becomes a real problem — any diets and restrictions quite often lead to serious breakdowns, which causes a "swing".

The weight that you have lost so hard returns with the Supplement again, so next time you will have to limit yourself even more and suffer even more-and so on without end.

Sooner or later, any overweight person understands that you need to completely change your approach to food, change your eating habits in order to achieve a sustainable result and keep it for a long time. For example, you can find out what foods cause a decrease in appetite, so that it is easier to change your diet — more precisely, so that you can there was no desire to eat too much in principle, including forbidden caloric and sweet foods, with which the weight increases as if by leaps and bounds.

1. Green tea

Two substances help you lose weight-catechin and caffeine. Together, they accelerate fat burning and boost your metabolism. It is important to remember that too much you should not get carried away with green tea — too much caffeine can cause problems with blood vessels and blood pressure. The maximum allowed amount is up to half a liter of drink per day.

2. Yogurt without sweetener and additives

This product is rich in protein, which saturates quite quickly. Except in addition, high-quality natural yoghurts contain a large number of live probiotic bacteria that help to improve digestion. You should avoid low-fat yoghurts — they may contain sugar and starch.

3. Lentils

Lentils are rich in folic acid, iron, manganese and potassium it is irreplaceable in the diet of those who refused to eat meat for various reasons. There are very few calories in lentils and even those who feel completely uncomfortable in the kitchen will cope with its preparation.

4. Spinach

The main value of spinach for losing weight is thylakoids, they increase the level of leptin. This particular one the hormone controls the feeling of hunger. Just one plate of spinach per day gives a very good effect.

5. Eggs

Eggs are also an excellent source of protein with relatively few calories. It is best to eat them for Breakfast, then the body will immediately adjust to a useful scheme nutritions.

6. Cucumbers

They are very high in water and fiber and very low in calories. It is best to combine cucumbers with fresh herbs, low-fat cottage cheese or natural yogurt or legumes. But it is better to avoid salt, so as not to cause fluid accumulation in the body.

7. Oily fish

For to achieve rapid weight loss, it is best to eat fish with steamed vegetables or green salad, instead of salt, use lemon juice and herbs.
