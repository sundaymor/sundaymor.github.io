How to pump up the Press at Home.

Hello all friends! I am sharing my training experience with You again.

Many friends often ask me "how come you always have abs? But no, it's not about genetics, it's about training and nutrition.

1. Nutrition, balanced nutrition is the main factor of a strong and relief press, without it You at least do not train but still do not achieve anything but a hernia. I am not saying that You should go on a strict diet and eat vegetables and fruits, but it is just necessary to understand your food, carbohydrates and proteins. A little more about BZHU is described in my last article . Now I will only say that just reduce your carbohydrate intake and significantly add protein (boiled eggs, cottage cheese at night, chicken, etc.). Protein food will help you build a relief faster press.

2. These are Workouts, they must be correct, change your training complex every time so that the press experiences stress, and the stress will be from something unusual, the more diverse each time your training, the better. Let's say today the upper abdomen, tomorrow the bottom, the day after tomorrow oblique muscles, planks and most importantly additional weight.

My program is 3-4 exercises per day with a total duration of 10-20 minutes with extra weight.

Do not strive to do on the quantity, do it efficiently, if you do more than 20 times take extra weight, even a bottle of water or plastic bottles. The extra load increases your muscles, not the multiplicity of repetitions. Try this option and I am sure you will make progress. Proper nutrition and proper training is the way to success.
