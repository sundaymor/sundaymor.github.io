Why do I eat an egg before Breakfast every day? I will tell you about one moment in weight loss, which I did not attach importance to, but in vain

Today, I will tell you about one useful tip, the so-called life hack for losing weight. I love boiled eggs and often eat them for Breakfast, but one point exactly for weight loss I never I took into account and did not attach any importance to it, and as it turned out, very much in vain.

Perhaps someone will use my experience, and they will also use this technique.

I've often wondered if it's possible to mix different colors squirrels?

Someone will say that it is possible, and someone that it is not...but, what about the reality?

It turns out that physiologically, it is better not to mix proteins of course. It is worth eating one type of protein. For our body, this is more convenient, because then it will only produce enzymes for one protein and digest it much faster better.

In this case, the exchange of substances will work "like crazy".

For example, you can eat 3 eggs, and you will be completely full.

But, I prefer to mix proteins and do it very often. I know it's not good, but I have a life hack)))

What I do.

For example, if I know what I have left a little chicken, but this amount of protein is not enough for me, and I try to eat protein only in the morning, then after I drink tea or coffee, I wait for 15 minutes and eat another egg or two.

Then, I exercise or go for a walk, and the next protein I eat is clearly 20-30 minutes after that.

Then that's it the amount of daily protein is usually consumed.

Or I do it like this. I eat one egg before going to wash, so I never overeat for Breakfast, and I can even postpone it an hour or two later and eat the main protein for lunch.

In General, just one egg in the morning is mine a little help-stick for the whole day. The body gets the necessary protein and starts its metabolism.

I can eat an egg with mustard, it is very delicious, but it will suit those who do not have problems with blood vessels.

In short, we can say this: if you want to lose weight, then eat protein for Breakfast, it will start your metabolism.

Egg contains the most protein, so if you eat it for Breakfast, then everything will be as it should be, and if you eat chicken protein for Breakfast, then it alone will not be enough and it is still worth eating an egg.
