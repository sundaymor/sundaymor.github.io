How to stay slim and not get fat? I talk about my diet

My dear reader! Welcome to my channel "about beauty in secret"! Let's talk today about how not to get fat and stay slim. The problem of excess weight is simply the number 1 among women, everyone tries very hard presentable look and each in its own way to strive for it.

Personally, I am against dieting, because even if the diet helps you lose weight, after you start eating as usual, the weight can return. And why do we need it? We are all different and what will be the optimal solution for one person, then for another person this isn't it at all. The gym and proper nutrition help me stay slim.

In the gym, I train with a trainer. But as for nutrition, I have accumulated knowledge that I have drawn from books, from coaches and from my own experience. I'll tell you what I know. Perhaps for whom it will not be new I don't know how much information I can provide, but my knowledge will still be useful to someone. 

The basic rules that I follow are as follows.

I drink 2 glasses of water after when you Wake up, not immediately, but with an interval of 15-20 minutes. I drink pure water on an empty stomach, do not add anything like lemon and honey, and do not advise, this will entail certain adverse consequences for the body. If you like water with lemon, drink it during the day, the main thing is not on an empty stomach. 

In the same way during the day I always I drink a glass of water about 18-20 minutes before eating. I drink about 2 liters of water a day. If you want to lose weight, this is an important condition. I don't drink food. After eating, I can drink tea or water for at least an hour. 

I always have protein, fat, and carbohydrates for Breakfast. I like omelets with vegetables, for example with broccoli. Protein is good for satiation, and fiber helps them digest better. In General, I always try to eat protein with fiber, especially for dinner.

I like meat or fish with vegetables, and no carbohydrates, no side dishes. Garnish is present on my table only for lunch. As snacks for me are fruits, or a glass of natural yogurt with nuts or dried fruits, or just a handful of nuts. I gave up sugar 12 years ago.

If I want something sweet, then I bake myself something from PP-recipes, adding sweetener to the dough instead of sugar, I use Fitparad, and I replace the usual flour with wholegrain. Well, I allow myself to eat only 1-2 slices a day, if it is some kind of cake or cake. I eat chocolate, but only bitter without added sugar and with a cocoa content of at least 80% a day, I can afford 3-4 slices from a bar of chocolate.

I love to cook cheesecakes and homemade oatmeal cookies with nuts. As for cottage cheese, I take 5%, there is no use in skimmed. By the way, if you want to lose weight, then consider that cottage cheese is a product with a high insulin index and you can eat it strictly until 17-18 hours. This is my main rule in nutrition, to monitor the glycemic index of products.

At I have a list of products with metrics. I only eat high-GI foods in the morning. Thanks to this, the extra centimeters on my waist do not bother me now. My last meal is three hours before bedtime.

Since I try to go to bed before 23h, my dinner takes place approximately in the morning 19: 30-20h. I eat 5 times a day, and 3 hours pass between my meals. A day I try to eat about 500 g of vegetables, if it does not work out, then I add fiber to natural yogurt.

I eat simple carbohydrates until 12h of the day. Complex meals for Breakfast and lunch. Nuts are eaten up to 15 hours. Lactic acid products up to 17-18 hours. And for dinner as I said above protein+fiber. I add linseed oil 1 tsp to the porridge, or I drink 1 tsp after Breakfast. I try to eat half an avocado every day. 

This is my diet , my nutrition, which I have been going to for years. I'm used to eating like this and there are no breakdowns.
