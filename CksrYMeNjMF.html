4 stages of the Dukan diet, nutrition rules, menus and calculations

The now popular Dukan diet is considered a real miracle of this century. It has helped a huge number of people achieve the desired result. It was created by Pierre Dukan, a very well-known nutritionist with a huge experience and more than thirty years of experience years.

Dukan has written nineteen books, with an astounding sales record. With the help of a simple and at the same time ingenious diet many representatives of the weak year achieve the ideal weight and get rid of the hated cellulite's.

The specific purpose of the diet is to minimize the risk of returning to weight before treatment, thanks to an optimally designed diet.

Description and principles of the Dukan diet

While following the Pierre Dukan diet, you will need to follow a few simple rules. But by sticking to them you can soon there is no time to notice really visible changes in appearance and well-being.

The description of the diet is quite simple and yet convincing. The human body can do without carbohydrates and fats for quite a long time. But at the same time, it constantly needs proteins, since they contribute to normal growth functioning.

Therefore, the nutritionist suggests switching to protein nutrition for a while, excluding almost completely the other two elements. At the same time, there are no restrictions on the time of eating and its quantity. You can arrange a late dinner after the work day. In this case, the portion may be any.

The basis for observing this diet is to increase the protein content in the daily diet while limiting the intake of fats and carbohydrates. Such a diet can easily be called protein or protein-based.

It is worth noting that in the course of its application, there are no restrictions regarding the amount of consumption of food and its frequency – each person decides individually, optimizing them for the needs of their own body. It is necessary to simultaneously consume a large amount of water, which contributes to detoxification of the body and helps in weight loss.

The diet is divided into several stages, gradually, changes are made to the daily diet. At the first stage, it is allowed to take only protein products, which in subsequent stages are supplemented with vegetables, fruits and other products.

The end result is a return to the traditional diet while simultaneously allocating one day when they eat only protein foods. Similar recommendations are aimed at consolidating the achieved effects and overcoming the occurrence of the yo-yo effect.

Four stages of the diet, compiled by Pierre Dukan.

The Dukan diet: attack

During this period, you will have to eat a food rich in protein. The duration of the stage the Dukan diet attack depends on how many extra pounds a thinning person has.

  • Ten kilograms – three days.
  • From ten to twenty kilograms – from three to five days.
  • Twenty-thirty kilograms – from five to seven days.
  • From thirty kilograms or more – from five up to ten days.

The duration is always individual, but it is not difficult to calculate it. During this stage, you can get rid of two to six extra pounds.

Diet menu

The first category is low-fat Meat products fat.

The second category is Offal.

The third category is fish With the lowest caloric content.

The fourth category is seafood.

The fifth category is rabbit and poultry Meat.

The sixth category is ham of low-fat varieties without skin.

The seventh category is Chicken eggs.

The eighth category is dairy Products. This is cottage cheese, milk, cheese, and low-fat yogurt.

In this case, you will have to meet several necessary conditions.

  • You should drink at least one and a half liters of liquid per day. It is worth noting that coffee, tea and milk are also included in this number.
  • Daily eat one and a half tablespoons of oat bran.
  • Take at least twenty-minute walks and exercise every day.

The Dukan diet: alternating

This stage is considered more difficult because you'll have to keep track of the order of days. On the Dukan diet, alternating consists of alternately eating either protein alone, or protein and vegetables.

During this period, there is a stabilization of body weight, further gradual weight loss and the breakdown of cellulite cells. The duration of the stage consists of two to six months. It is best to alternate the days one through one.

- 125 grams of low-fat fruit yogurt.

— A tablespoon of corn starch or soy flour.

— 30 grams of cheese with a fat content of no more than 7%.

- 40 grams of processed cheese up to 7% fat.

— A tablespoon of sour cream with a fat content of not more than 3%.

— A teaspoon of bitter cocoa 11 percent fat content.

For the attack phase — a tablespoon per day.

For the alternating phase — on protein days one tablespoon, in protein – vegetable days-two tablespoons.

For the fixing phase-no more than three tablespoons per day.

It is worth remembering that during the alternation completely prohibited pasta and dishes from cereals.

The Dukan diet: fixing

In this phase, there is a gradual transition to a more diverse set the diet menu and maintaining the achieved weight loss.

The duration of this period on the Pierre Dukan diet directly depends on the number of pounds lost. One kilogram is equivalent to ten days of the Dukan diet phase of consolidation.

Diet menu

  • 100 grams of bread from whole-grain flour.
  • 40 grams of any cheese whose fat content does not exceed forty percent.
  • One serving of any fruit per day, except bananas, cherries, figs, cherries and grapes.

It is allowed to eat two hundred grams of any berries, a slice of fresh watermelon or any other fruit whose size does not exceed higher than the orange.

Twice a week, you can eat a small amount of bacon or lamb meat.

Two hundred grams of pasta. This is the weight of a ready-made portion. Butter is forbidden, it is better to Supplement macaroni with onions and tomatoes.

220 grams of lentils.

125 grams of potatoes, baked in foil.

220 grams of any legumes.

200 grams of wheat groats.

125 grams of rice.

It is important to remember that it is forbidden to take a Supplement for yourself. On holidays, you can afford a portion of the first and second and a little dessert. But it is better to replace it with a small amount of wine and slice of cheese.

In the third period, you should devote one day a week exclusively to protein food. It's best if it's Thursday. The amount of bran in the diet increases to twenty-five grams. Walking time increases to at least half an hour, but it is better to walk as much as possible any longer.

Stabilization

This is the final stage, at which you can see almost the final result of the diet. It is during the Dukan diet that you will have to strictly observe several very important rules.

The transition to an acceptable diet. Do not forget those rules of the nutritionist, which were discussed in the third phase. Vegetables and protein can be eaten in unlimited quantities. Fruit only once a day, some cheese, potatoes and bread made from rye flour.

Be sure to leave one pure protein day in the week.

Drink water and coffee as often as possible and tea, at least two liters in total per day.

Walking for at least twenty minutes should be daily.

Walk up the stairs as often as possible, forgetting about the Elevator and escalator.

The habit of eating three tablespoons of oat bran should remain for life.

To do any sport.

Advantages of the Dukan diet-efficiency and durability

The undoubted advantage of this diet is its effectiveness, as well as the process of consolidating results. It is worth noting that unlike other weight loss diets, the Dukan diet does not require fasting and does not seem to be too much strict.

By providing the body with the necessary vitamins and minerals, you can maintain health while losing weight.

Disadvantages of the diet

The main drawback of this diet is the excessive amount of protein consumed. Using it in large quantities can lead to the appearance of medical consequences, in the form of stress on the kidneys and liver. The body becomes excessively acidified, and a decrease in the fiber content in the diet is fraught with digestive disorders and the formation of constipation.
