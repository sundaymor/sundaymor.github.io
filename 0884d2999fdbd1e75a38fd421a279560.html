7 REASONS TO CHECK YOUR VISION

OCCASION #1

Dryness, redness and excessive fatigue of the eyes are a cause of anxiety and anxiety for many, especially for residents of large cities, whose work is associated with long-term work on a computer or tablet close professional monitoring of devices, working with documents,

Reason #2

Seasonal dryness, tearfulness and redness of the eyes can cause vision loss. Do not think that dryness in the eye, excessive tearfulness, puffiness and redness of the eyelids — this is only a momentary effect environment. If the problem does not resolve, although there is no strong wind, frost or bright sun, and you thought that this is the reason, then it is time for a consultation, and maybe for treatment to an optometrist or allergist. You should not hope for a beautiful advertisement of another tool, which is so tempting to pour from the screen TV'S. Proper prescribing of medications combined with special exercises will help protect your eyes.

OCCASION #3

How often do we put short-term pain in the eyes and a haze, when everything around us is seen as if through water, down to fatigue. Flashing "stars” or" flies” are also not visible once flew before my eyes. It seems that this is just overwork, and a short rest or vacation will bring relief, but no. This condition is repeated again and again, so it is time to immediately go to the doctor. It is possible that the optometrist will refer you to a neurologist, because this may manifest symptoms of others diseases not related to the eyes. Deterioration of blood supply to the eye or a certain area of the brain, abuse of substances that affect blood flow, retinal vascular pathology, corneal dystrophy can also be the root cause of such complaints.

REASON #4

Ophthalmological pathologies associated with abnormal refraction, for example, nearsightedness, farsightedness and astigmatism — is a serious reason not only for a one-time visit to an optometrist, but for regular visits to a specialist. Predisposing factors — lack of vitamins and trace elements, bacterial or viral eye diseases, genetic predisposition.

The main task of the doctor is to assess the dynamics of the pathological process and the selection of appropriate corrective glasses or lenses.

REASON #5

The feeling of loss of full vision, narrowing of the angle of vision, different clarity of vision in the eyes, feeling of a film on the eye-these are signs cataracts. With age, the symptoms only get worse, and you should not think that it will pass by itself. If for some time these symptoms do not leave you, you should urgently consult a doctor. There is no cure for cataracts; only surgery can help.

Today, ophthalmology is a highly developed industry you will be able to get professional help from a specialist in a modern ophthalmology center. Cataract removal is a fairly simple procedure, with which you can achieve 100% restoration of vision by removing the cloudy lens and implanting a new, artificial one.

OCCASION №6

Constant pain in the eyes should alert the patient. This can be a symptom of insidious and dangerous glaucoma. The disease is characterized by an increase in intraocular pressure and the development of damage to the optic nerve with the loss of basic eye functions, up to blindness. Only a specialist, having measured the intraocular pressure, able to make a diagnosis and start treatment. Self-medication in this case is just as dangerous as in other attempts to restore or improve vision independently.

OCCASION #7

The latter sign can be detected by looking at photos, when you accidentally see white highlights on the eye. Appearance such a "photographic" symptom indicates that the visual organ sends a sad signal of trouble. This is how cataracts, leukocoria, toxocariasis, and retinoblastoma of the retina can manifest themselves. All this is a reason for an immediate consultation with a specialist.
