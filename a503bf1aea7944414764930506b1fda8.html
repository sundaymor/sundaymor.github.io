Hollywood diet. (Alkaline)

This diet is popular not only because most Hollywood stars adhere to it, but also because it does not harm health, and on the contrary improves the condition of the skin, hair, nails, promotes cleansing of the body, and increases immunity.

The main rule of the diet is to maintain an acid-base balance in the diet. All food products are divided into 3 types: alkaline, acidic and neutral, depending ON the pH compliance

0-7 acidic

7 neutral

7-14 alkaline.

Deviation of the acid-base balance both in one direction and in the other can be dangerous for health. Most often, the deviation goes in the direction of acidification, this is facilitated by the use of animal food, the more acidified our body is, the more diseases it can provoke.

Except diseases also have such consequences as slowing down the metabolism, hormonal failures,weight gain.

One of the alkalizing products required for this diet, one of the simplest and most easily available, is water.

Following this diet for a month helps you lose up to 5 kg of weight, and improve your body.
